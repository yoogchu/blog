bio

Hello –

i’m eugene, currently an undergrad studying cs at georgia tech. i climb, i take pictures, and i write. sometimes i take pictures of my climbing, and sometimes i write about my pictures. i guess it all blends together. i used to have life goals and shit on this page but i read it and it makes me want to gag.

i used to write for others, now i write for me. it’s not great, but i’m working on it. also trying to be more wholesome and real – i’ve been on the other side and i’m glad to be back. if you read my blog, please don’t tell me. don’t bring up a blog post, don’t bring up a certain sentence i wrote. all posts are spontaneous and what i felt at the time. if you bring it up, i will probably die a little inside. Thanks and please enjoy the ramblings of a millenial just trying to make it in this big, big world.
