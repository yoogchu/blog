bio

Hello –

I’m currently a 4th year studying at Georgia Institute of Technology. I’m majoring in Computer Science with a minor in Aerospace Engineering. Looking down the road, I hope to do meaningful work with artificial intelligence. Humans are prone to error. Humans crack under pressure. By implementing artificial intelligence into various aspects of daily life, I believe that overall quality of life can be increased.

In my free time, I have a passion for rock climbing, specifically bouldering. I appreciate and enjoy the duality of challenges that it presents me: physically and mentally. Seeking and solving routes through creative solutions and then implementing those solutions with hard work and determination provides me with extraordinary satisfaction.


People say follow your passion. My passion isn’t necessarily something I can specifically follow, however, more of a philosophy: to never stop learning. If I had to attribute my switch in majors, 5 semesters in, to anything, it would be due to computer science being so readily available to learn. There’s just so much available information out there, that makes learning topics on algorithmic sorting, machine intelligence, whatever, infinitely more accessible compared to other fields.
