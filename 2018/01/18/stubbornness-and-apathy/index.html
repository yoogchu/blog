stubbornness and apathy

I got a V6-7 today at the gym! I realized that a fundamental part of my drive is my stubbornness – applicable to climbing, and other aspects, i suppose. Walking away means defeat – and i won’t let myself be bested by a rock, of all things. Last year, I used to climb and if i fell off, id think, “not today..” But this asks, no, begs the question, why not today?
