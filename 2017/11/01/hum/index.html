hum

it’s nights like this where i sorely miss for our summer nights. i want to make my illegal u-turns, pick you up from nouvelle, and stroll down westpark. i yearn to go our, i guess, now-finished highgate, and sit on the balcony’s edge. i long to hold your hand and squint at the stars, gazing at the heavens above that i struggled to see.

yet i endure, i suppose, and unsuccessfully try to do my work, waiting – waiting.. impatiently, for the next thursday i get to see you.
