crossroads

Sometimes things happen so fast in life, I feel like I just kept swept along without really questioning if it’s what I truly desire. I’ve never been one to back down from a challenge, or to dwell too long in my comfort zone. People around me are doing great things, reaching higher, pushing themselves. But here i am; what am i doing? It’s not a competition, its not a race, I constantly remind myself. I have a difficult time accepting that because I’ve always been motivated by doers, by those that seek greatness. I don’t want to be surrounded by normality.. complacency is where creativity and drive go to die.
