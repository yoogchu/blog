last first day

Today was my last first day of school! If we’re counting by year, and not by semester. Definitely will be my most difficult year in terms of balancing credits (20!!!), interviewing, job prep. I’m dangerously optimistic though – for once. It’s strange. My past 3 years, I’ve never been particularly proud to call myself a Yellow Jacket, but I think now I am. With an offer in hand, I feel less stressed in my classes, but of course I still want to do well. I think I’m going to try more for actual learning instead of just grades.


Also this morning I, ok wow, it just hit me where I was this morning. I spent the weekend in nashville for my 21st and i honesly couldn’t have asked for a better time. AMAZING people, good food (+1 for pf changs), 100% totality on eclipse, and, of course, nicole.
