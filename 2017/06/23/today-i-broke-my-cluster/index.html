today i broke my cluster

Today, at work, I broke my EMR cluster, which I thought was pretty hilarious. There were a bunch of permissions set (to prevent someone from doing something like this) and I, being the smartass that I am, unashamedly thought I was better than that. How dare you prevent me from writing to root access?? I’ll show you!!
Long story short, I executed “sudo chmod -R 777 /usr/*” and did a little sudo chown’in, and completely broke sudo. Couldn’t even set the permissions back to root ownernship, either, cause I didn’t have the power. Heh. Ended up pushing up all my files to github, and then terminating the cluster.

Anyway, other than bricking these environments, I’ve been doing pretty well. I think in Atlanta, I always had a certain restlessness that stems from my copious free time. Here, I appreciate the exhaustion of work, and the people around me. I feel like there’s never enough time.
