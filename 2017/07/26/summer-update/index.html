summer update

So.. it’s week 7 of 10 here at capital one. And i can’t believe how much i’ve enjoyed my summer, seriously. Coming in, i was looking forward to it but didn’t expect that much. I never expect that much, but thats not necessarily how I want to think anymore. hmm. Little bit about my project: using apache spark on an emr cluster to do batch processing for customer account information. sounds lame but when the code actually works (when i actually have a usable cluster), MAN do i feel a rush of excitement.

I’ve also been climbing A LOT. And i don’t know if i’m getting better or not cause i’m not actually finishing most of the climbs i attempt, but i’m trying new things which is good. Been trying a lot of V6-V8s and I can get decently far!! Idk. I’m glad i found a climbing buddy this summer cause i feel like its a lot easier to burn out when you climb by yourself.

Photography-wise – it’s been interesting. My motivation definitely shifted gears a bit in regards to my 100 days. I realized that the most enjoyable aspect was capturing the highlight of my day, so when i look back, i can remember what happened then. Its difficult though – ever since meeting nicole, she’s always been the highlight of my day. And i’ve always been one to not do things just for the sake of doing things!! So instead of posting random pictures that didn’t really capture how I felt that day, I’ve decided to put it on a hiatus. Maybe one day I’ll return, but for now… now, i’ll just enjoy.

I guess that leads to the most exciting part of my summer – where to begin. i dont really know how it happened, just that i’m glad it happened. happiness beyond ordinary happiness. Hmmm. don’t really want to talk too much about it cause its still early. A parting thought: i really hope the drive from atlanta to nashville is scenic.
