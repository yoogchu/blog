wholesome friends

hi heather, i hope you read this because you’re my inspiration for this post.
First of all, i didn’t realize how much I missed your company. driving home, i felt an inner peace that i haven’t felt in a long, long time.

Thank you.

I think it’s important for friends to abandon their tough facade every once in a while and really take time to appreciate each other. Banter is fun, and company is good. But sincerity pierces the bullshit of society in the most refreshing way.
I’ve been interested in photography for a while now and, over the last semester, really put in effort towards improving. Nobody really showed interest in my photos, so I kind of just tapered off recently. Last night, my good friend hit me with this:


A stupid wide smile plastered my face and I teared up with joy.
I don’t know; maybe I’m being melodramatic over something small. But honestly, that recognition filled me with an ethereal, glowing happiness that enveloped me entirely.

Thank you. Thank you so much.
