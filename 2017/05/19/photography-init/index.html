photography init

Today marks my first serious attempt at photography, I think. I’ve always liked taking pictures ever since I had my Motorola Razr, but this is my first “real” camera. I’ll be starting my journey with the Sony a6300 and from playing around with it all day, I really like it. There’s a lot of things I don’t know but I’m keen to learn along the way.

Something fascinating about photography is that by really immersing myself, I’ve come to appreciate the aesthetic of daily life. I dont know, maybe I’m being pretentious. But the other day, I was driving down a country road I’ve driven down countless times before and this time I felt like I was experiencing something special.
On that train of thought, I also believe photography requires a skilled eye, a creative vision. Maybe the scene isn’t anything special, and maybe the lighting is really poor, but perhaps the corner of that building lines up just right and you just know that there’s something special there.

On a less inspirational note, I really hope to have more posts about photography and what I’ve experienced along my journey. Also I’m working on a section for my photos, perhaps on my main site. But we’ll see.
