learning that i suck

The more I learn about cs, the more I realize that I suck. I don’t even think it’s a linear relationship – probably expotential.

After ~1wk of working on my little photo gallery, I thought it was in a pretty decent condition despite taking forever to load. Upon using Google’s pagespeed tool, it literally gave me a score of 0/100 and I couldn’t help but chuckle.
Through this, I learned how different companies use progressive image loading to create a decent user experience. I was also curious about how Instagram loads images so fast so I poked around a little, and I thought it was extremely interesting how they begin uploading photos to their servers before user even submits the photo. This was such a stroke of genius, truly mind-boggling to me.

In regards to the cs world, I feel like a mouse travelling a labyrinth, perhaps not explicity looking for cheese – just exploring. At every turn, I discover greater depth and complexity.
I’m excited to keep exploring.
