will

I often see people do things they don’t necessarily want to, or follow stupid rules just because. And I think to myself, “Grow some balls, man.” Why would you do ever do ANYTHING that you don’t want to, if it makes you miserable? Because society tells you to? Because you’re following some unwritten rule somewhere that you have to do this in order to be successful?

No.

I say do what you truly want, what you desire, if you’re willing to accept the consequences. See that’s the thing. Nobody is putting a gun to your head and forcing you to do anything. Most people do it because they’re afraid. But once you get over that fear, I think, is when the opportunities for happiness truly become limitless.
